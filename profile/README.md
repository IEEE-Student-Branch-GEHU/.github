## 🌍 About Us

IEEE SB GEHU is a student-driven technical community focused on:

- Open Source Contributions
- AI & Machine Learning Projects
- Hackathons & Competitive Programming
- Technical Workshops & Webinars
- Research & Innovation

We aim to foster technical excellence, collaboration, and innovation among students.
